Agbada

Agbada is more than just a garment, it is a symbol of pride, culture, and sophistication in African fashion. Traditionally worn by men, this flowing robe is characterized by its intricate embroidery and vibrant fabrics, making it a staple for special occasions, celebrations, and cultural events.

Kaftan

The kaftan is a timeless garment that embodies elegance, comfort, and cultural richness. Originating from various regions across Africa and the Middle East, this versatile piece has evolved into a beloved staple in contemporary fashion. At Ab's Couture, we celebrate the kaftan as a symbol of style that transcends occasions, making it perfect for both casual outings and formal events.
